📘 How to Use This App

  • Click the moon to play intro music 🎵
  • Press "Let's Start" to begin the story 📖
  • Adjust music volume 🔊 using the slider
  • Tap 🔊 Speak to hear the story + animation
  • Tap 🔈 Stop to stop voice + show full story
  • When reading ends, screen breaks and restarts 🔁
